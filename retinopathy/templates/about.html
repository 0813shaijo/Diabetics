About This Project

The Silent Threat to Vision Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) damages retinal blood vessels. In its early stages, it presents *no symptoms*, allowing it to progress undetected until vision is irreversibly damaged. Our AI Detects What the Eye Can't See our system is trained to identify the earliest, most subtle biomarkers of the disease, long before a human might notice them.

  • Microaneurysms: Tiny bulges in blood vessels, the earliest sign of DR.
  • Hard Exudates: Lipid deposits indicating leaking blood vessels.
  • Hemorrhages: Small blood leaks within the retina.

By catching these early indicators, our AI empowers timely intervention, helping to preserve vision and prevent blindness.

Proprietary CNN Model
Trained on a diverse, international dataset of over *70,000 retinal images*, ensuring high accuracy across populations.
Utilizes advanced convolutional neural network (CNN) architecture optimized for medical image analysis.
Proven Performance
Achieves a consistent and validated overall accuracy of approximately *85%* in detecting referable diabetic retinopathy.
